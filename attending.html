Registration Links

Registration Opens February 2, 2021
Early Registration Deadline March 16, 2021
Regular Registration Deadlinee April 13, 2021

This year, CHI 2021 conference will be done virtually, presenting us with an opportunity for reduced registration rates for everyone, particularly for individuals residing in 139 economically developing countries who register during the early registration period.

There are three categories for early registration: C, H, I. Individuals belonging to the H and I category are eligible for the reduced registration rates. The countries listed in this page are from Asia only and can be found here.

Workshop registration fees are lower but we require attendees to register for at least one day of the conference. The conference registration fee increases significantly after the early registration deadline and only regular and late registration rates rates apply after the early registration has passed. In preparation for CHI 2021, delegates and everyone involved in CHI must abide to theACM policy against harassment and be aware of the support of SIGCHI CARES.
